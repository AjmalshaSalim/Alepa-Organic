Multi floral
Alepa Organics Multi Floral Honey is naturally harvested and delivered to you pure with no additives.
Natural multi-flora honey, also known as multi flower honey, is produced by honeybees that often feed on a variety of springtime flowers. It is created from the nectar of many floral species.
A study suggests that honey helps to treat upper respiratory infections in children.
Antioxidants present in multiflora honey can prevent inflammation in the body that are a primary cause of major health issues, including cardiovascular complications, cancer, and autoimmune diseases.
